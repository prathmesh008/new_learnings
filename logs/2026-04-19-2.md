## 2026-04-19 — TypeScript generics

Okay so generics today, man. Been re-reading a bit and it finally clicked why `K extends keyof T` is so important. Like before I kinda got it, but today I properly grokked how it’s not just *a* key type, it literally tells TS that `K` *must* be one of the actual keys on `T`. It's what makes stuff like `const getProp = <T, K extends keyof T>(obj: T, key: K) => obj[key];` actually work with proper inference. Blew my mind a bit, honestly. Way more powerful than I thought.
