## 2026-05-14 — React useCallback vs useMemo

Okay so I've been messing with `useCallback` vs `useMemo` all afternoon, trying to grok when to actually use which. For ages I just thought "function goes in `useCallback`, anything else in `useMemo`." But today it kinda clicked that `useCallback` is just `useMemo` when your *value* is a function. Like, `useCallback(myFunc, [deps])` is basically just a shortcut for `useMemo(() => myFunc, [deps])`. Dunno why that wasn't obvious earlier. Made me feel a bit dumb, but also kinda cool to connect those dots finally.
