## 2026-04-26 — database sharding

Okay so sharding. Man, that's a whole thing. But it clicked today that the whole point is just to split your data across multiple servers so one machine doesn't get totally swamped. The trickiest part seems to be picking the actual "shard key," though. Like, if you shard by `country` and 90% of your users are from "USA", that one USA server is gonna be completely hammered while others are just chilling. Total hot spot. Gotta pick something that distributes evenly, like `user_id` probably.
