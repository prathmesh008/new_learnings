## 2026-05-15 — Docker volumes

Okay so, docker volumes. Spent way too long on these today. The big thing that finally clicked is how they actually *persist* data even when the container's long gone. Before I'd just lose everything if I rebuilt a container, which was just annoying. So `docker run -v myapp-data:/var/lib/mysql ...` means my precious database stuff doesn't vanish into the ether. Makes so much sense now why that's even a thing.
