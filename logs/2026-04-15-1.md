## 2026-04-15 — WebSocket internals

Okay so been reading about websockets today, trying to finally grok how they *actually* work under the hood. The whole handshake part confused me for ages, like how does it just *become* a different protocol? But it clicked today. It literally starts as a normal HTTP request, like 'GET /chat HTTP/1.1' but then it sends a `Upgrade: websocket` header, and if the server's cool with it, boom, it switches. Then it's just raw frames flying back and forth, not HTTP packets anymore. Wild.
