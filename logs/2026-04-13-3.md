## 2026-04-13 — Express middleware chain

okay so today, middleware chain finally clicked for me. spent ages wondering why my request sometimes just *hung*. turns out if you dont call `next()` in a middleware, the whole thing just… stops. totally freezes. like, i was expecting express to just magically pass on, but nope. unless you `res.send()` or something, you *gotta* `next()`. for real, `app.use((req, res, next) => { console.log('logging'); /* no next here */ });` will just kill the flow.
