## 2026-04-18 — Redis data structures

ugh, redis hashes. been reading about them and it finally clicked why you'd even use them over just a bunch of individual keys. i kept thinking "why not just `user:1:name`, `user:1:email`?" but no, it's about grouping all those fields under *one* key, `user:1`, then accessing the fields. `HSET user:1 name "Alice" email "alice@mail.com"` is way cleaner and prob saves memory too, all in one go. makes sense now, less fragmented.
