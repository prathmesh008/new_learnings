## 2026-05-04 — debounce vs throttle

Okay so, debounce vs throttle finally clicked. Was always mixing 'em up. It's like, debounce waits for *silence* – you stop typing for 300ms, then run the search, y'know? Like that `clearTimeout` then `setTimeout` pattern. But throttle? Nah, that's just "run this at most every 200ms" no matter what, just limits the *rate*. My brain kept wanting to make debounce also rate-limit, but it's rlly about the *pause*. Big difference for, say, a resize event vs an autocomplete.
