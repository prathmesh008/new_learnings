## 2026-04-29 — rate limiting strategies

Okay so I was digging into rate limiting today and fixed window seems so simple on the surface, like, "100 requests a minute, done." But then it clicked – the problem isn't the total, it's the burst right at the window edge. Everyone could hit 99 times at 00:59, then 99 times at 01:00, basically 200 requests in like, two seconds. That's why sliding window is so much better, tracks individual request timestamps. Gotta try implementing that with Redis, `ZRANGEBYSCORE key (timestamp - window_size) timestamp` is the move.
