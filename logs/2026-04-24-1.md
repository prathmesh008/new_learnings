## 2026-04-24 — React useCallback vs useMemo

Okay so today I finally wrapped my head around `useCallback` vs `useMemo` in React, like *really* wrapped it. For ages, I just kinda thought they were... similar? But no, the lightbulb moment was realizing `useCallback` is literally just `useMemo` when what you're trying to memoize *is* a function itself. So, instead of `useMemo(() => myFunc, [deps])`, it's just `useCallback(myFunc, [deps])` to keep that function reference stable. Duh. Makes so much sense now for passing down to children to stop pointless re-renders. Feel kinda dumb it took this long lol.
