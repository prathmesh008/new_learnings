## 2026-05-15 — PostgreSQL indexing

Okay so been looking at `EXPLAIN ANALYZE` output today, trying to figure out why some queries are still kinda slow even with an index on `user_id`. Saw "Bitmap Heap Scan" a few times and for a sec I was like, wait, is it even using the index? Turns out it totally is, just scanning the index for the row IDs then efficiently fetching the actual data blocks from the heap. It's like a two-step process, which makes sense for when you select more columns than just the indexed one. So `CREATE INDEX ON users (user_id)` totally helps for specific lookups. Good to know.
