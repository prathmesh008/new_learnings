## 2026-05-19 — MongoDB aggregation pipeline

Okay so aggregation pipelines man, they're finally clicking. Today it hit me how much better `$addFields` is than just using `$project` all the time for new calcs. Like, `$project` forces you to list out every single old field you wanna keep, which is a massive pain bc I usually just wanna add *one* new thing. But `$addFields`? Just `{$addFields: { "totalPrice": {$sum: "$items.price"} }}` and all the original docs are preserved. Huge sigh of relief, way less verbose.
