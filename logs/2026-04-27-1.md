## 2026-04-27 — Docker networking

okay so finally wrapping my head around Docker networking a bit better. The default bridge network is cool and all for single containers, but man, custom bridge networks are where it's at for multi-service apps. Realized today how crucial that internal DNS resolution is when you're spinning up multiple services. Just slap `--network my-app-net` on your `docker run` commands and suddenly your backend can find the database by its container name. No more messing with internal IPs, it's just so much cleaner. Felt kinda dumb not getting that sooner, tbh.
