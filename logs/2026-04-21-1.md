## 2026-04-21 — Git rebase vs merge

Okay so, finally grokking why people say `git pull --rebase` is better sometimes. Like, for my own feature branch, before pushing, if origin updated, doing a merge just adds this extra merge commit. But `rebase` just moves my commits on top of the new `main` commits, keeping everything linear. No messy diamond shapes. Super clean history for personal stuff. That really clicked.
