## 2026-04-28 — CAP theorem

Okay so been reading about CAP theorem today, and like, the big click for me was realizing P isn't optional? You *will* have network partitions eventually, it's just a fact of life for dist systems. So you're basically always picking between Consistency and Availability. Like with Cassandra vs a strong RDBMS cluster, if a node goes down Cassandra keeps taking writes even if it's eventually consistent, but a strong system might just stop serving requests on that part. Crazy stuff.
