## 2026-04-22 — SQL query optimization

Okay so I finally properly looked at `EXPLAIN ANALYZE` today and holy shit. All this time I've been guessing why queries are slow, but this just lays it all out. Like, literally shows the "Cost" and "Rows" and "Width" and you can pinpoint the bottleneck. Ran it on that super slow report query `SELECT user_id, count(*) FROM orders WHERE order_date > '2023-01-01' GROUP BY user_id;` and it was clear the `GROUP BY` was killing it. Kinda mad I didn't get this tool earlier, would've saved so much time.
