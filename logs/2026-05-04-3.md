## 2026-05-04 — message queues

Okay so, message queues. Finally getting my head around why they're so damn useful, especially for background tasks. The whole "don't wait for a response" thing? That clicked today. Instead of my main app blocking forever while some image resize task chugs along, I just fire off a message to a queue and move on. The worker picks it up whenever, totally async. `channel.basic_publish(exchange='', routing_key='image_processing', body=img_id)`. Such a cleaner way to decouple stuff, feels good.
