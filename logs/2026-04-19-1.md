## 2026-04-19 — NestJS dependency injection

Ugh, NestJS DI. Still wrap my head around it sometimes. Today was trying to make a custom logger and got stuck on `useFactory` in the providers array. For ages I thought it was just for constants or super simple stuff. But then it clicked – you can literally inject *other* providers into the factory fn! Like, put `ConfigService` in the `inject` property, and boom, your factory gets config. Wild. That makes so much more possible for dynamic setup.
