## 2026-04-15 — environment variables best practices

Okay, so env vars today. The whole `.env` file thing, I get it, `git ignore` for sensitive stuff. But for like, production? It finally clicked that you don't just *copy* the `.env` file to the server. Nah, you set them up directly on the hosting service, like Netlify or Vercel, or even just `export API_KEY=abc` in the shell before running your app. That's why you need defaults or separate files for dev. Makes so much more sense now why my deployed app was failing trying to read from a non-existent .env. Total facepalm moment.
