## 2026-05-13 — nginx reverse proxy

Okay so, Nginx reverse proxy stuff, man. Spent like an hour today trying to figure out why my backend app kept thinking it was `localhost` instead of the actual domain. Like, wtf? Turns out, Nginx just forwards the request as is sometimes, so the app had no idea what domain it was serving. Had to explicitly tell it `proxy_set_header Host $host;` inside the location block. Boom. Suddenly everything just worked. So dumb it took me that long to realize.
