## 2026-04-14 — Express middleware chain

Okay so, express middleware chain, man. I always kinda just *used* it, ya know? But today it really clicked that if you forget `next()` in one of your middleware functions, the whole thing just STOPS. Like, the request literally hangs there forever, waiting for a response that never comes. Made a quick test route: `app.use((req, res, next) => { console.log('first'); /* next(); */ }); app.get('/', (req, res) => res.send('ok'));` and boom, nothing. Wild. Gotta be super mindful of that `next()`.
