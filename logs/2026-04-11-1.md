## 2026-04-11 — Git rebase vs merge

okay so, finally clicked a bit on rebase vs merge. Before, I just merged everything, whatever, no biggie. But now, seeing how `git rebase main` just squashes your changes *on top* of the latest main commit, it's just so much cleaner for a pull request. Makes it look like you were always working from the most recent code, instead of having those extra merge commits. Less visual noise in the history, y'know? Makes sense why ppl do it.
