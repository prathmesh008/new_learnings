## 2026-05-24 — Docker networking

Okay so Docker networking. Finally getting my head around custom bridge networks, like, duh, they're so much better than the default one. The big thing that clicked today was how containers on the *same* custom network can just talk to each other by their service name. No more messing with `--link` or trying to find IPs. So if I have `docker run --net my-app-net --name webapp` and then a `db` container on the same net, `webapp` can just hit `db:5432`. That's actually huge for microservices stuff. Makes life way easier.
