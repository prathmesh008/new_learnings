## 2026-06-08 — database transactions

Ugh finally clicked on transactions today. Been reading about them forever but today it just hit me, the whole "all or nothing" thing is *so* important. Like, preventing half-done operations. If you're transferring money, you absolutely CANNOT have it leave one account but never arrive in the other, that's a nightmare. So you gotta `BEGIN; UPDATE sender...; UPDATE receiver...; COMMIT;` or if something breaks, `ROLLBACK;` and it's like nothing ever happened. Phew.
