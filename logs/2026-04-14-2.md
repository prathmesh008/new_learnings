## 2026-04-14 — nginx reverse proxy

okay so today it finally clicked why `proxy_pass http://localhost:3000/` vs `proxy_pass http://localhost:3000`. like, that trailing slash makes a HUGE difference for how nginx rewrites the path, legit kept getting 404s. I'd hit `/api/users` and nginx would just drop the `/api` part and send `/users` to the backend when it had the slash. Wild. Such a small detail but it broke everything. Always thought it was just a style thing.
