## 2026-06-06 — database transactions

okay so transactions, man. Been diving into them and it finally clicked why they're not just "nice to have" but essential. Like, imagine a money transfer – you debit, then credit. If the credit fails, you can't just leave it, right? That's where the whole `BEGIN; COMMIT; ROLLBACK;` pattern comes in. All or nothing, bro. If something goes sideways, just `ROLLBACK;` the whole damn thing like it never happened. So neat.
