## 2026-05-20 — CAP theorem

Okay so been reading about CAP theorem today, and it finally clicked like, it's not just a general pick-two thing, it's specifically about what you prioritize *when a network partition happens*. That was the part I kept missing. Like, if you're building something and you pick AP, you're saying "screw consistency if the network flakes out, keep serving stuff." Thinking about DynamoDB as a classic AP example, like sure eventually it'll sync up but for a bit you might read stale data. Made way more sense after that.
