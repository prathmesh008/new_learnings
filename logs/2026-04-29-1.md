## 2026-04-29 — API versioning strategies

Okay so been reading about API versioning today, like how you stop breaking everyone's stuff when you update your backend. The whole URL versioning thing (`/api/v1/users`) just clicked as super practical. Like, yeah, header versioning (`Accept: application/vnd.myapi.v2+json`) feels a bit more 'proper' or RESTful whatever, but for simple testing in a browser or even curl, putting `v1` right there in the path is just so much easier to see what you're hitting. No weird headers to remember, just change the URL. Makes debugging way less of a headache when you're just messing around.
