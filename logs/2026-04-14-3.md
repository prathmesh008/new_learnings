## 2026-04-14 — debounce vs throttle

okay so debounce vs throttle, man that tripped me up for ages. today it finally clicked why you'd pick one over the other. like, debounce is for when you *stop* doing something, right? think search input, only fire the API call once they're done typing, not for every single letter. total `setTimeout` energy. throttle is more like, okay, you *are* doing something continuously, but just don't overwhelm the system, like for scrolling or resizing, just fire an update every 200ms or whatever, no matter how fast they scroll. makes so much more sense now for perf.
