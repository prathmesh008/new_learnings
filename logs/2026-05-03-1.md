## 2026-05-03 — nginx reverse proxy

Okay so I finally figured out why my app was getting 404s for css and js when going through nginx. It was the stupid `proxy_pass` trailing slash! If you do `proxy_pass http://backend:8080/app;` (no slash), it basically *replaces* the `location` path. But if you do `proxy_pass http://backend:8080/app/;` (with the slash!), it just sends the *remainder* of the path. Ugh, such a small detail but wasted hours. My conf now has `proxy_pass http://my_service:3000/;` and everything's working.
