## 2026-06-04 — database sharding

Okay so, sharding databases. For real, it finally clicked why it's not just about splitting up a huge table, but how you *design* the split. Like the sharding key is everything, dude. If you shard by `user_id`, all a user's stuff stays together. Super important for queries but then what if one user just blows up? Still a bit fuzzy on how you'd rebalance that without downtime. Wild stuff.
