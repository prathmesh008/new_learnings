## 2026-04-17 — NestJS dependency injection

okay so the whole NestJS DI thing finally clicked today. i kept forgetting why i needed `@Injectable()` on my `UsersService` if that service didn't *itself* have dependencies. but duh, it's not about *its* deps, it's about letting Nest know *it* can be injected elsewhere! like, into my `UsersController`. so now `constructor(private readonly usersService: UsersService) {}` just works, nest figures it out. felt a bit dumb for missing that for so long lol.
