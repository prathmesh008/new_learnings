## 2026-04-17 — WebSocket internals

okay so been deep diving into WebSocket internals today, especially the framing. And the masking key? Bro, that confused me for a sec why it even existed. Then it clicked – ONLY client-sent messages are masked to the server, the server *never* masks its replies. It's literally just XORing the payload with a 4-byte key like `data_byte ^ masking_key[i % 4]`. Mind blown that the server doesn't bother with it.
