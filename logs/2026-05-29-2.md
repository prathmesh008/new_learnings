## 2026-05-29 — API versioning strategies

Okay so API versioning. Been reading about it. The `Accept` header thing, like `Accept: application/vnd.myapi.v2+json`, that just clicked. Before I was like, why not just put `/v2/` in the URL? But this way, the URL stays clean, and the client specifies what they want *without* changing the whole endpoint path. Kinda elegant. Less re-writes on client side if they just upgrade their header for a new version, def easier to manage. Just update that one header.
