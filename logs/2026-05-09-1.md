## 2026-05-09 — database sharding

Okay so sharding, man. Been reading about it. The whole `hash(user_id) % num_shards` thing for distributing data finally clicked, it's not just about splitting up a huge table, it's about making sure your *queries* don't have to hit every single machine. Like, if you always query by `user_id`, that's a perfect shard key. But then I started thinking about global queries, like "show me all active users" and uh oh. That's where it gets messy. Kinda feels like you're trading one problem for another, just a different kind of problem, you know?
