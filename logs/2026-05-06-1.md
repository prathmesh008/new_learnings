## 2026-05-06 — REST vs GraphQL

Okay so been reading about REST vs GraphQL again, trying to really grasp the practical differences. And honestly, the whole 'over-fetching' thing with REST just clicked today. Like, before, I always just made my `/users` endpoint and thought nothing of it. But what if I only need `username` and `email` for a simple list? REST just dumps *everything* from the user object, their address, phone, whatever. GraphQL's like, just ask for `users { id username email }` and that's *all* you get. So efficient. Crazy how much unnecessary data can be flying around otherwise.
