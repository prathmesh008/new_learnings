okay so rate limiting strategies, like, proper ones. not just a dumb counter. spent ages today trying to grok sliding window and how it handles bursts better than fixed. the big click finally was how you even *implement* tracking timestamps across servers without it being a nightmare. redis sorted sets, duh. `ZADD user:123:requests (timestamp) (timestamp)` then just `ZREMRANGEBYSCORE` for old ones. makes so much sense for not just dropping a bunch of requests right at the window end.
