## 2026-05-04 — PostgreSQL indexing

Okay so been digging into Postgres indexes today and man, it's not just a free lunch for reads. Like, I always knew they helped with `SELECT`s but totally forgot about the write overhead. Every `INSERT` or `UPDATE` means the database has to update the index too, duh. My `users` table with 100k rows, adding an index on `email` totally sped up lookups but now I'm thinking about those nightly bulk imports. It's not *just* the table growing, it's the index growing too. Makes sense now why you gotta be smart about where you put 'em.
