## 2026-05-17 — database transactions

Okay so transactions finally clicked today. Been reading about them for ages but the whole "all or nothing" thing really hit home when thinking about bank transfers. Like, if you debit one account, you HAVE to credit the other, or both operations gotta fail. No in-between state, ya know? That's what `START TRANSACTION;` and `COMMIT;` are for, or `ROLLBACK;` if something goes sideways. Makes so much sense for data integrity.
